NTYouTube lets you download YouTube videos as .mp4 files.

All you do is turn a youtube.com URL into an ntyoutube.com URL.

If you want a video in HD, add &hd=1 to the end of the URL.

(If you're using NoteTote, adding an ntyoutube.com URL to your download note will make NoteTote download the video.)
